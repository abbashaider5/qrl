Our Story

Established in 2017, Quick Luxury Ride Car Service has been at the forefront of exceptional luxury transportation. Our mission is to provide travel defined by professionalism, reliability, and unwavering dedication.

We handpick chauffeurs and train them rigorously to ensure they have the skills, experience, and commitment required to serve our clients.

Punctuality is a cornerstone: whether for critical business meetings or special occasions, we ensure you reach your destination without delay.

Excellence

Our luxury vehicles are meticulously maintained for comfort and safety—ranging from sleek sedans to spacious SUVs.

We tailor every journey to individual needs, striving to exceed expectations each time you ride with us.

Choose Quick Luxury Ride for sophistication, comfort, and reliability guided by elite chauffeurs and an impeccable fleet.

Based in New York, serving NYC and surrounding areas with premium black car service.
